NUTRIENT RICH

We value ingredients that are in synchronicity with planet Earth. Our ingredients must be sourced ethically and sustainability and they must do no harm to people, planet or animals. Read about our no-go list here.

CRUELTY FREE

We not only refuse to test our products on animals, we also choose ingredients that are strictly not tested on animals and we don’t use animal products anywhere in our range.In our opinion, there is no need for the use of animal products in cosmetics.

NOTHIN TO CHANGE

Mica is the beautiful shimmering mineral that gives Aleph products glow and colour pigment. There is a substantial child-labour problem in the mica mining industry, however we ensure the mica we source is child-labour

To all of us at Aleph, sustainability isn’t just about doing no harm. It’s about doing more good. That’s why we make informed, conscious, constructive choices across every aspect of our business every day.
